I have always been passionate about foreign languages and I thrive on this sector through the translation of English and Italian documents. After following a Foreign Languages Degree path, I obtained a Master’s degree in Technical Translation in 2010.

I then moved to Italy where I worked for 7 years in a translation agency. During this period, I translated and proofread documents of different types, managed translation projects and gained the terminology related to various areas. During all these years, I enriched my language and immersed myself into the culture of the country.

Today, I work as a freelance translator, specialising in the areas of Cosmetics, Tourism & Travels and Marketing, but I also offer services in many other technical fields.

Working method

Whatever the nature of the document, I translate with the utmost care, while respecting your confidentiality, your requirements and the agreed deadlines. I reproduce the essence of your messages faithfully into my mother tongue, making sure that I adapt them to the culture of my country.

Education

Foreign Languages Degree, with specialisation in Translation, University Rennes 2 - Haute Bretagne, FRANCE.

Master’s degree in Technical Translation, University Rennes 2 - Haute Bretagne, FRANCE.
