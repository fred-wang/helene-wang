Translating involves an excellent knowledge of the language of the source text in order to understand the message in all its subtleties and to perceive its chosen tone. It also requires a familiarity with the subject and in-depth knowledge of the terminology specific to the sector.

The golden rule is to translate into one’s mother tongue. Why? Because the mother tongue is the one we know best, the one which cradled us, the one with which we grew up with. Its nuances, its subtleties and its cultural elements don’t have any secrets for us. The result is a fluid and natural text where the influence of the source text isn’t felt in the structure of the sentences or in the choice of words.

Proofreading

I check your texts in French comparing them with the source document in order to verify the exactness of the translation. I correct each mistake, such as errors related to typos, omissions, grammar, orthography, punctuation and syntax. I also amend any stylistic mistakes modifying the structures of the sentences to produce a more fluid text. Finally, I fix the layout if there are any differences compared with the source text and I check that the typographic rules of the French language have been applied.

Audio/Video Transcription

I provide a transcription service of your French audio or video files (interviews, reports, meeting speeches…). Based on your preferences, the transcription can be delivered in Word, TXT or Excel format. You can choose between two types of transcription:

  • Integral transcription: this is a faithful reproduction, word-to-word. This transcription includes all the hesitations, repetitions, onomatopoeias…
  • Proofread transcription: this is a reformulation of the speech into a written style (suppression of the repetitions and the hesitations, corrections of the syntax errors…). This proofread transcription produces a fluid text and is easier to read.
